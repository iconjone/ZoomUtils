Howdy! Let's get started
The app is loaded with default meetings. To get rid of them, select the delete icon (or open the menu tab), and select the entries to delete or type 123456789. Be sure to toggle delete again, to get out of delete mode.
You can then click (or open the menu tab), the add icon and then fill out the appropriate inputs.
After your meetings are added, you may drag and drop the entries in which ever way you would like them to appear!
Lastly, Click on the Meeting ID to launch the ZOOM app!
If adding via link, Zoom Utils will attempt to find the meeting ID. If failed, please manually navigate to your link in your browser, and observe the link generated. After 'zoom.us/j or w or s/' you should see a 9 to 11 digit id which is your Meeting ID!
